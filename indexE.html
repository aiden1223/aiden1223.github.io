Shilin Night Market-Recommended food

新竹家鄉碳烤雞排

No. 1, Jihe Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

Price：80 NTD each
The biggest and most special selling point of this chicken steak is that the chicken steak is fried first and then grilled, with special sauce containing special Chinese Herbal Medicine. The chicken itself is juicy and tender。

  • Go

郭家蔥油餅

No. 113, Wenlin Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

Price：20 NTD each（with egg 30 NTD)
This time-honored Guo Jia Scallion Pancake is a popular snack. The fried method is actually a bit like Hualien's Fried Egg Scallion Pancake,tastes crispy。

  • Go

蕭記碳烤肉捲

The intersection of Dadong Road and Danan Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

Price：12 NTD each
The simple piece of meat is wrapped with green onions and grilled on fire. The gravy that bursts in the mouth after eating the food blends with the aroma of green onions. The seemingly simple taste is quite enjoyable!

  • Go

家湘涼麵

No. 49, Danan Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

The cut-out boxy stinky tofu, golden and crispy, has the same tenderness and kimchi as the inside. !! And Thai hot and sour cold noodles are also the signature here. , Both come with a plate, double enjoyment! !!

  • Go

阿輝麵線

No. 67, Danan Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

No. 84, Danan Road, Shilin District, Taipei City

Normal University Night Market-Traffic Information

捷運(MRT)

1.Take the Danshui Line to Guting Station and exit at Exit 4. After exiting, follow Heping East Road to Shida Road and walk to Shida Night Market for about 10 to 15 minutes。
2.Take the Xindian line to Taipower Building Station and exit at Exit 3. After exiting the station, turn right and walk straight along Shida Road. It takes about 5 minutes to reach Shida Night Market。

公車(Bus)

take 18、 74、 235、 237、 278、 662、 663、 672、 907、 和平幹線Get off at “Shida University” or “Shida University 1” station on Heping East Road。

開車(Car)

1.Take the Zhongshan Expressway, under the Yuanshan Interchange, take the second section of Jianguo North Road to South Jianguo Road, turn right to Heping East Road to Heping East Road, and left to Shida Road (via the headquarters of Taiwan Normal University)。
2.North Second Expressway, turn Muzha Interchange to Wanfang Interchange, go down Xinhai Road to Xinhai Road, turn right to Roosevelt Road to Roosevelt Road, turn right to Heping East Road, turn left to Shida Road。
